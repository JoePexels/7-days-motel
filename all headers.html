Information

FAQs

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our resort and services.

What time is check-in and check-out? +

Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and additional charges.

Do you offer airport transfers? +

Yes, we offer complimentary airport transfers for guests staying in our suites. For other room categories, airport transfer services are available at an additional charge. Please contact our concierge to arrange your transportation.

Is the resort child-friendly? +

Absolutely! We welcome families and offer a range of child-friendly amenities including a kids' club, children's pool, and special menus. Baby-sitting services can also be arranged with advance notice.

What dining options are available? +

We have three restaurants and two bars on property, offering a variety of culinary experiences from fine dining to casual poolside snacks. Room service is available 24 hours for in-room dining.
