Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in time is from 2:00 PM, and check-out time is until 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request and subject to availability.

Do you offer airport transportation?

Yes, we offer complimentary airport shuttle service from Entebbe International Airport, which is just 5 minutes away. Please inform us of your flight details in advance.

Is parking available at the motel?

Yes, we provide secure complimentary parking for all our guests. Our parking area is monitored 24/7 for your safety and convenience.

Do you have facilities for business travelers?

Absolutely! We offer a business center, meeting rooms, high-speed internet, and executive rooms with work desks to cater to the needs of business travelers.
