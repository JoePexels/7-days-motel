Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and facilities

What are your check-in and check-out times? +

Check-in time is from 2:00 PM, and check-out time is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and additional charges.

Do you offer airport transfers? +

Yes, we offer complimentary airport transfers for guests staying in our premium suites. For other room categories, airport transfer services are available at an additional charge. Please contact our concierge to arrange your transportation.

Is parking available at the motel? +

Yes, we provide secure, complimentary parking for all our guests. Our parking facility is well-lit and monitored 24/7 for your peace of mind.

Do you have facilities for business travelers? +

Absolutely! We offer a business center with printing, copying, and fax services. All our rooms have dedicated workstations and high-speed WiFi. Meeting rooms can also be arranged upon request.

What dining options are available? +

We have multiple dining options including our fine dining restaurant, casual bar and lounge, and 24/7 room service. Our coffee corner serves premium Ugandan coffee and fresh pastries throughout the day.
